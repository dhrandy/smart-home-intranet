Other Smart Home Devices

Not all the Smart Home devices connect to the SmartThings hub or even work together. Some work together through an app called IFTTT and others not at all. For example, the Nest Protect smoke detectors and the MYQ Garage door opener neither connect with other smart devices or IFTTT. At one time they did, but no longer do.

There's also other devices that are smart devices that don't have connectivity in the house. For example, the upstairs spare bathroom. It uses motion detection to turn on the light.
