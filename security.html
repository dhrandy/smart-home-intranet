Security

The SmartThings hub controls security in our house. We use Life 360 to auto arm and disarm our security system. At night we use the Alexa command "Alexa, Goodnight" to arm the night security and in the morning we use the Alexa command "Alexa, Good morning" to disarm our night sercurity.

You'll need the Life 360 app setup to auto-arm and auto-disarm the security as you come and go from our house.

Devices Used For Sercurity

  • Door Sensor on all exterior doors.
  • Motion Sensor downstair
  • Motion Sensor Upstairs
  • Motion Sensor in Master Bedroom
  • Master Bedroom Door Sensor
  • Master Closet Door Sensor
  • Arlo Cameras
  • Canary Cameras
