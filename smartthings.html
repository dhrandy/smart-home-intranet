SmartThings

What is SmartThings you may ask? SmartThings is the central hub that connects all the smart switches, smart lights, and smart plugs as well as the Samsung TV and Vacuum. It also integrates with the Ring doorbell and arlo cameras. You can control the smart devices through the app or from Alexa.

You'll need access to the app to control devices manually.

SMARTTHINGS HUB

Devices SmartThings Can Control

  • Living Room Samsung TV
  • Fireplace
  • Living Room Lights
  • Kitchen Lights
  • Samsung Vacuum
  • Roomba Vacuum
  • Ring Doorbell
  • Leak Detectors
  • Thermostat
  • Front Door Lock
SMARTTHINGS APP

Devices SmartThings Can't Control

  • Canary Camera's (inside)
  • Echo Devices
  • Nest Smoke Detectors
  • Bathroom Lights
  • Bathroom Fans
  • Back Door or Inside Garage Door Lock
  • Garage Door
  • Garage Camera
